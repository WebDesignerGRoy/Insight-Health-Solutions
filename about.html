About Insight Health Solutions

We exist because the systems designed to protect clinicians were built to respond — not to prevent.

Our Story

From reactive to predictive

Insight Health Solutions was founded on a simple observation: healthcare continuously monitors patient outcomes but does not continuously monitor clinician stability. This gap leaves institutions, patients, and clinicians themselves exposed to preventable harm.

Other high-stakes industries — aviation, military, nuclear energy, professional sports — solved this problem decades ago through continuous predictive monitoring. Healthcare trails behind, relying on wellness surveys, compliance checklists, and post-incident reviews that catch problems only after damage has occurred.

Our team of physicians, behavioral health experts, and data scientists built the infrastructure that didn't exist: a predictive performance intelligence platform that integrates directly into operational workflows, identifying destabilization patterns before they become crises.

Our Values

What guides every decision we make
